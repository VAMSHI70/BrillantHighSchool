                        School Achievements

National Science Olympiad Champions

Our school's science team achieved an extraordinary feat by winning the prestigious National Science Olympiad. Competing against schools from across the nation, our students demonstrated exceptional knowledge and skills in various scientific disciplines.

  • Secured First Place in Robotics

This remarkable achievement reflects the dedication and hard work of our students, who spent countless hours preparing and refining their skills. It is a testament to their unwavering commitment to excellence and their passion for science.

Our triumph in the National Science Olympiad not only highlights the talent and capabilities of our students but also showcases the exceptional quality of education and support provided by our school.

Indian Talent Olympiad Champions

Our students showcased their exceptional talent and intellect by securing top honors at the prestigious Indian Talent Olympiad. Competing against participants from across the nation, our students excelled in various subjects, displaying their profound understanding and mastery of academic disciplines.

  • Overall Best School in Indian Talent Olympiad
  • First Place in Mathematics Category
  • Winner of the Science Round

This remarkable achievement highlights our commitment to nurturing academic excellence and fostering a culture of intellectual curiosity among our students. It is a testament to the dedication and hard work of both students and teachers in pursuit of excellence in education.

Our success in the Indian Talent Olympiad not only reinforces our position as a leader in academic excellence but also serves as a source of inspiration and motivation for future generations, encouraging them to strive for greatness and reach new heights of achievement.

International Art Exhibition Participants

Students from our visual arts program were selected to participate in an international art exhibition, where they showcased their exceptional talent and creativity. Their works received acclaim for their innovative and thought-provoking nature, captivating audiences and earning recognition from esteemed judges.

  • Curator's Choice Award for Mixed Media
  • People's Choice Award for Sculpture
  • Excellence in Painting Recognition

Receiving the Curator's Choice Award for Mixed Media, the People's Choice Award for Sculpture, and the Excellence in Painting Recognition is a testament to the artistic prowess and dedication of our students. Their ability to express themselves through various mediums and techniques speaks volumes about their passion for the arts and their commitment to excellence.

Participation in the international art exhibition not only provided our students with a platform to showcase their talent on a global scale but also enriched their artistic journey, inspiring them to continue pushing boundaries and exploring new creative horizons.
